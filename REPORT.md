## 🔍 Key Patterns This Cycle

### Dominant Opportunity Type: Vertical AI Agents
The #1 and #2 opportunities are both vertical AI agents targeting SMBs. The pattern: pick an industry with high manual work + low software penetration + clear ROI, build an AI agent for their specific workflow. Healthcare, real estate, and professional services are the richest verticals.

### Subscription Is King
74% of opportunities (48/65) are subscription-based. This is expected — recurring revenue is the most capital-efficient model for solo operators. The average target price point: $49–$999/mo.

### AI Infrastructure Is the Enabler
Nearly every opportunity uses 3–5 of: Vapi/Retell (voice), Whisper (transcription), Claude/GPT-4o (reasoning), Plaid (financial data), Twilio (messaging), and a vertical API (AthenaHealth, Shopify, Google Ads). The stack to build most of these is now commoditized.

### Regulatory Complexity = Opportunity
The highest-scoring niches often involve regulatory complexity: SOC 2 compliance, prior authorization, healthcare documentation, ESG reporting, import/export. Complexity deters competition and creates pricing power.
